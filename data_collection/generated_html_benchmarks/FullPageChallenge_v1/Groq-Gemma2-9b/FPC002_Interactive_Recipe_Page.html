A decadent and rich chocolate lava cake recipe that will impress your guests.

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 12-15 minutes
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour four ramekins.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and sugar.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and whisk until smooth.
  4. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is still slightly soft.
